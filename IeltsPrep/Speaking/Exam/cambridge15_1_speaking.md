# IELTS Speaking Part 1 – Emails (Band 7.5+ Sample Answers)

## 1. What kinds of emails do you receive about your work or studies?

**Answer:**

Since I'm a software engineer, I mainly receive work-related emails about project updates, meeting schedules, and technical discussions. I also get emails from online learning platforms about new programming courses and software development trends. Occasionally, I receive notifications about job opportunities and industry events, which I find quite useful for my professional growth.

**Useful Vocabulary**
- work-related emails
- project updates
- meeting schedules
- technical discussions
- online learning platforms
- software development trends
- job opportunities
- industry events
- professional growth

**Useful Phrases**
- I mainly receive...
- I also get...
- Occasionally...
- I find it quite useful...

---

## 2. Do you prefer to email, phone or text your friends? Why?

**Answer:**

I definitely prefer texting because it's quick, convenient, and doesn't interrupt the other person's routine. My friends and I usually communicate through messaging apps to share updates, arrange plans, or send funny memes. However, if there's something important or urgent, I prefer making a phone call because it's more personal and efficient.

**Useful Vocabulary**
- texting
- convenient
- messaging apps
- share updates
- arrange plans
- urgent
- personal
- efficient

**Useful Phrases**
- I definitely prefer...
- One of the main reasons is that...
- However...
- If it's something important...

---

## 3. Do you reply to emails and messages as soon as you receive them? Why or why not?

**Answer:**

It depends on the situation. If the email or message is work-related or urgent, I usually respond as soon as possible because I don't like delaying important tasks. However, if it's not time-sensitive, I may reply later when I have enough time to give a thoughtful response instead of rushing.

**Useful Vocabulary**
- work-related
- urgent
- time-sensitive
- delaying
- thoughtful response
- rushing

**Useful Phrases**
- It depends on the situation.
- I usually respond as soon as possible.
- On the other hand...
- If it's not time-sensitive...

---

## 4. Are you happy to receive emails that are advertising things? Why or why not?

**Answer:**

Not really. Most promotional emails are irrelevant to my interests, so I usually ignore or delete them without reading. However, I don't mind receiving advertisements from technology companies because they sometimes introduce useful software, gadgets, or discounts that genuinely interest me.

**Useful Vocabulary**
- promotional emails
- irrelevant
- ignore
- advertisements
- technology companies
- gadgets
- discounts

**Useful Phrases**
- Not really.
- I usually ignore...
- I don't mind...
- They sometimes introduce...

# Band 7.5+ Answer Framework

### Question Type: Preference

**Structure**

Opinion → Reason → Example

Example:
> I prefer texting because it's quick and convenient. It allows me to communicate whenever I have free time. For instance, my friends and I usually use WhatsApp to make plans.

---

### Question Type: Frequency

**Structure**

Frequency → Reason → Example

Example:
> I usually reply to emails as soon as possible because I don't like keeping people waiting. However, if I'm busy, I respond later after finishing my work.

---

### Question Type: Opinion

**Structure**

Direct answer → Explanation → Personal example

Example:
> Not really. Most advertising emails aren't relevant to my interests, so I normally delete them. However, I occasionally read emails about technology products because they can be genuinely useful.
