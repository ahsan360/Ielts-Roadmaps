# IELTS Speaking Part 1 – Emails (Band 7.5+ Sample Answers)

## 1. What kinds of emails do you receive about your work or studies?

**Answer:**

Since I'm a software engineer, I mainly receive work-related emails about project updates, meeting schedules, and technical discussions. I also get emails from online learning platforms about new programming courses and software development trends. Occasionally, I receive notifications about job opportunities and industry events, which I find quite useful for my professional growth.

**Useful Vocabulary**
- work-related emails
- project updates
- meeting schedules
- technical discussions
- online learning platforms
- software development trends
- job opportunities
- industry events
- professional growth

**Useful Phrases**
- I mainly receive...
- I also get...
- Occasionally...
- I find it quite useful...

---

## 2. Do you prefer to email, phone or text your friends? Why?

**Answer:**

I definitely prefer texting because it's quick, convenient, and doesn't interrupt the other person's routine. My friends and I usually communicate through messaging apps to share updates, arrange plans, or send funny memes. However, if there's something important or urgent, I prefer making a phone call because it's more personal and efficient.

**Useful Vocabulary**
- texting
- convenient
- messaging apps
- share updates
- arrange plans
- urgent
- personal
- efficient

**Useful Phrases**
- I definitely prefer...
- One of the main reasons is that...
- However...
- If it's something important...

---

## 3. Do you reply to emails and messages as soon as you receive them? Why or why not?

**Answer:**

It depends on the situation. If the email or message is work-related or urgent, I usually respond as soon as possible because I don't like delaying important tasks. However, if it's not time-sensitive, I may reply later when I have enough time to give a thoughtful response instead of rushing.

**Useful Vocabulary**
- work-related
- urgent
- time-sensitive
- delaying
- thoughtful response
- rushing

**Useful Phrases**
- It depends on the situation.
- I usually respond as soon as possible.
- On the other hand...
- If it's not time-sensitive...

---

## 4. Are you happy to receive emails that are advertising things? Why or why not?

**Answer:**

Not really. Most promotional emails are irrelevant to my interests, so I usually ignore or delete them without reading. However, I don't mind receiving advertisements from technology companies because they sometimes introduce useful software, gadgets, or discounts that genuinely interest me.

**Useful Vocabulary**
- promotional emails
- irrelevant
- ignore
- advertisements
- technology companies
- gadgets
- discounts

**Useful Phrases**
- Not really.
- I usually ignore...
- I don't mind...
- They sometimes introduce...

# Band 7.5+ Answer Framework

**Structure**

**Structure**

Frequency → Reason → Example

Example:
> I usually reply to emails as soon as possible because I don't like keeping people waiting. However, if I'm busy, I respond later after finishing my work.

# IELTS Speaking Part 2 (Band 7.5+)

## Describe a hotel that you know.

### Sample Answer

I'd like to talk about a hotel that I know quite well called **Grand Sylhet Hotel & Resort**, which is located near **Osmani International Airport** in **Sylhet, Bangladesh**. Although I haven't stayed there overnight, I visited it for a family gathering and had the opportunity to explore most of its facilities.

From the outside, the hotel looks extremely modern and elegant. It has a striking glass façade, a spacious entrance, and beautifully landscaped gardens that create a welcoming atmosphere. The lobby is bright and luxurious, featuring comfortable sofas, stylish décor, and a magnificent chandelier. As soon as I entered the hotel, I was impressed by its cleanliness and sophisticated interior design.

In terms of facilities, the hotel offers almost everything a guest could ask for. It has spacious and well-furnished rooms equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi, air conditioning, smart televisions, comfortable beds, and modern bathrooms. There are also several restaurants serving both local and international cuisine, a large swimming pool, a fully equipped fitness centre, a relaxing spa, and conference halls for business meetings and social events. Moreover, the staff members are professional, polite, and always willing to assist guests, which creates a pleasant experience.

Overall, I believe this is an excellent hotel to stay in. It combines luxury, comfort, and convenience in one place. Its location near the airport makes it particularly suitable for tourists and business travellers. Although staying there can be quite expensive, I think the outstanding service and high-quality facilities make it worth every penny.

If I ever have the chance, I would definitely like to stay there for a few days because I believe it would be a relaxing and memorable experience.
