# Part 1: Spending and Saving Money

## 1. When you go shopping, do you usually pay for things by cash or by card?

I usually pay by **card** or through **mobile banking** because it's much more convenient than carrying cash. It also allows me to keep track of my expenses, which helps me manage my budget more effectively. However, if I'm shopping at a small local store, I sometimes pay in cash.

---

## 2. Are you generally careful about how much money you spend?

Yes, I'd say I'm quite careful with my spending. Before buying anything, I usually ask myself whether I really need it or if it's just an impulse purchase. I prefer spending money on things that provide long-term value rather than wasting it on unnecessary items.

---

## 3. Have you ever spent money on something you didn't need?

Yes, definitely. A few months ago, I bought an expensive pair of wireless earbuds simply because they were on sale. At the time, it seemed like a great deal, but later I realised my old earbuds were still working perfectly. That experience taught me to think twice before making impulsive purchases.

---

## 4. How important is it to you to save money for the future?

Saving money is extremely important to me because it provides financial security and peace of mind. Unexpected expenses can arise at any time, so having savings helps me handle emergencies without unnecessary stress. Moreover, I want to save enough money to travel, invest in my career, and eventually buy a house.
