# IELTS Speaking Part 1 – Sample Answers (Band 7.5–8+)

## Question 1: What do you do? Are you a student or do you work?

**Answer:**

I'm a software engineer, although I still consider myself a student because technology is constantly evolving. That's why I regularly study topics like system design, which helps me keep my skills up to date.

---

## Question 2: Do you enjoy your job?

**Answer:**

Yes, I really enjoy my job because it allows me to solve real-world problems. Each project is different, which keeps my work interesting and engaging.

---

## Question 3: What activities do you enjoy in your free time?

**Answer:**

I usually spend my free time working on personal programming projects because they help me improve my technical skills. Whenever I build something new, I learn practical techniques that benefit my career.

---

## Question 4: How do you usually relax after a long day?

**Answer:**

After a long day, I usually watch tech videos or read technology blogs because I enjoy learning new things. It may sound like work, but I find it both relaxing and rewarding.

---

## Question 5: What kind of music do you like?

**Answer:**

To be honest, I don't usually listen to music because I find it distracting when I'm studying or working. Whenever I need to concentrate, I prefer a quiet environment, which helps me stay focused.
