## 📚 Vocabulary

| Word | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Persuasive | Able to convince people to do or believe something |
| Engaging | Interesting and capable of holding attention |
| Compelling | Extremely convincing or captivating |
| Intrusive | Annoying because it interrupts or invades privacy |
| Misleading | Giving a false or inaccurate impression |
| Memorable | Easy to remember |
| Authentic | Genuine and trustworthy |
| Consumer | A person who buys products or services |
| Brand awareness | The extent to which people recognize a brand |
| Value for money | Worth the amount of money spent |

# 🎯 Questions & Sample Answers

## ❓ 1. Do you like watching advertisements?

**Answer**

> Yes, provided they are creative and informative. I particularly enjoy advertisements that tell a compelling story or incorporate humour. However, I usually skip repetitive or overly promotional ones because they tend to become monotonous.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Creative
- Informative
- Compelling
- Humour
- Promotional
- Monotonous

---

## ❓ 2. Will you buy something because of an advertisement?

**Answer**

> Advertisements may capture my attention, but I rarely purchase a product solely because of one. I prefer to read customer reviews, compare different options, and ensure the product offers good value for money before making a decision.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Capture my attention
- Customer reviews
- Compare different options
- Value for money
- Purchase

---

## ❓ 3. How do you feel when you see pop-up ads on the internet?

**Answer**

> To be honest, I find them rather irritating because they interrupt whatever I am doing. They can also slow down websites and distract me from my original purpose. Therefore, I usually close them immediately or use an ad blocker.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Irritating
- Interrupt
- Distract
- Original purpose
- Ad blocker

---

## ❓ 4. Do you like funny or serious advertisements?

**Answer**

> I definitely prefer funny advertisements. Humorous commercials are far more memorable and entertaining, and they create a positive impression of the brand. Serious advertisements are suitable for important issues, but they are generally less engaging.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Humorous
- Memorable
- Entertaining
- Positive impression
- Engaging

---

## ❓ 5. What makes a good advertisement?

**Answer**

> In my opinion, a good advertisement should be concise, visually appealing, and informative. It should clearly highlight the product's benefits without exaggerating. Creativity and authenticity also play a significant role in making an advertisement memorable.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Concise
- Visually appealing
- Highlight
- Authenticity
- Significant role
