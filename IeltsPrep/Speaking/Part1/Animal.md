# ❓ Do you like animals?

> Yes, absolutely. I've always been fond of animals because they are fascinating and can bring a great deal of happiness to people's lives. I especially enjoy observing them in nature, as it helps me appreciate the beauty and diversity of the natural world.

---

# ❓ What is your favourite animal?

> My favourite animal is the cat. I like cats because they are affectionate, independent, and relatively easy to look after. Spending time with a cat is also a great way to relax after a busy day.

---

# ❓ What types of animals do you have in your country?

> Bangladesh is home to a wide variety of animals. We have domestic animals such as cows, goats, and chickens, as well as wildlife like deer, monkeys, crocodiles, and the Royal Bengal Tiger. We also have many species of birds and fish.

---

# ❓ What types of animals are kept as pets in your country?

> The most common pets are cats, dogs, birds, and fish. In recent years, rabbits and turtles have also become increasingly popular, especially among families with young children.

---

# ❓ Do you think animals should be kept in zoos?

> I think it depends on how the zoo is managed. If it provides proper care and contributes to wildlife conservation, it can play an important role in protecting endangered species. However, animals should always have enough space to live comfortably and behave naturally.

---

# ❓ Do you have any animals in your home as a pet?

> At the moment, I don't have any pets because I'm quite busy with my studies and work. I don't think I could give a pet the attention and care it deserves.

---

# ❓ Did you have a pet when you were a child?

> Yes, I did. I had a small cat when I was a child, and I was very attached to it. Playing with my pet was one of my favourite childhood memories because it always made me feel happy and relaxed.

---

# ❓ Would you like to have a pet in the future?

> Yes, definitely. If I have more free time in the future, I'd love to have a cat or a dog because they make excellent companions and can make a home feel much more lively.

---

# ❓ Do you have a pet?

> No, I don't have one at the moment. My schedule is quite demanding, so I wouldn't be able to take proper care of a pet. I'd rather wait until I have more free time.

---

# ❓ What types of animals do you think make the best pets?

> In my opinion, cats and dogs make the best pets because they are loyal, friendly, and capable of forming strong emotional bonds with their owners. They also provide companionship and help reduce stress.

---

# ❓ Why do people have pets?

> I think people keep pets mainly because they provide companionship and emotional support. In addition, looking after a pet teaches responsibility and can improve a person's overall well-being.

---

# ❓ Should dogs be left in the house alone while their owners are at work?

> Only for a short period. Dogs are social animals, so leaving them alone for too long may cause loneliness or anxiety. If owners have to work long hours, they should arrange regular walks or ask someone to look after the dog.

---

# ❓ Is there any type of animal that you think should not be kept as a pet?

> Yes. I don't think wild animals such as tigers, monkeys, or snakes should be kept as pets. They belong in their natural habitats, and keeping them at home can be dangerous for both the animals and the owners.
