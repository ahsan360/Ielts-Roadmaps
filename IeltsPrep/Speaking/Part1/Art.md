# 🎯 Questions & Sample Answers

## ❓ 1. Do you like art?

**Answer**

> Yes, I do, although I would not describe myself as an expert. I appreciate artworks that convey emotions or tell meaningful stories. Visiting art exhibitions occasionally helps me relax and broadens my perspective.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Appreciate
- Artworks
- Convey emotions
- Meaningful
- Broadens my perspective

---

## ❓ 2. Do you think art classes are necessary?

**Answer**

> Yes, I believe they are essential. Art classes encourage students to think creatively, express their emotions, and develop problem-solving skills. They also provide a welcome break from purely academic subjects.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Essential
- Think creatively
- Express emotions
- Problem-solving skills
- Academic subjects

---

## ❓ 3. How do you think art classes affect children's development?

**Answer**

> I think art classes contribute significantly to children's overall development. They enhance creativity, improve concentration, and strengthen fine motor skills. Moreover, they encourage children to communicate their thoughts confidently and think independently.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Contribute significantly
- Overall development
- Enhance
- Fine motor skills
- Think independently

---

## ❓ 4. What benefits can you get from painting as a hobby?

**Answer**

> Painting can be incredibly therapeutic. It helps reduce stress, stimulates creativity, and improves patience. In addition, completing a painting gives a great sense of accomplishment and boosts self-confidence.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Therapeutic
- Stimulates creativity
- Patience
- Sense of accomplishment
- Self-confidence
