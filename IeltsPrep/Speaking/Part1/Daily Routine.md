# 8. Band 7.5+ Sample Answers

## Q1. Tell me about your daily routine.

My daily routine is fairly structured because I like to stay organised. I usually wake up around seven in the morning, freshen up, and have breakfast before starting work. Since I'm a software engineer, I spend most of my day coding, attending meetings, and learning new technologies. In the evening, I exercise, practise English for the IELTS exam, and spend some quality time with my family before going to bed. Having a routine helps me stay productive and manage my time effectively.

---

## Q2. Has your daily routine changed since you were a child?

Yes, it has changed quite significantly. When I was a child, my days mainly revolved around school, playing with friends, and watching cartoons. Nowadays, my routine is much more structured because I have professional responsibilities and long-term career goals. I spend considerably more time working, learning new skills, and planning for the future than I did as a child.

---

## Q3. Is your daily routine different at the weekend than during the week?

Yes, definitely. During the week, I follow a fairly strict schedule because of my work and study commitments. At weekends, however, I have much more flexibility. I usually wake up a little later, spend time with my family or friends, and occasionally go out to explore new places or enjoy a meal. Even so, I still dedicate some time to learning and self-improvement.

---

## Q4. What would you like to change about your daily routine?

If I could change one thing, I'd definitely spend more time exercising and reading. My job requires me to sit in front of a computer for long hours, so I don't always get enough physical activity. I believe that exercising regularly and reading more books would improve both my physical well-being and personal development.

---

## Q5. Do you think it's important to have a daily routine?

Yes, absolutely. I think having a daily routine is essential because it helps people stay organised, disciplined, and productive. When you have a clear plan for the day, it's much easier to manage your responsibilities and avoid wasting time. At the same time, I believe it's important to remain flexible because unexpected situations can arise, and being adaptable is just as valuable as being organised.
