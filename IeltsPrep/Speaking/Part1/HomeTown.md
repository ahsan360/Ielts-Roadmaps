# ❓ Question 1

## Where is your hometown?

### ✅ Answer

> My hometown is Sylhet, which is situated in the north-eastern part of Bangladesh. It is one of the country's most picturesque cities and is well known for its lush tea gardens, rolling hills, and beautiful rivers. I have lived there for most of my life, so I know the city extremely well.

---

# ❓ Question 2

## What do you like about it?

### ✅ Answer

> What I appreciate most about Sylhet is its breathtaking natural beauty and peaceful atmosphere. Unlike many crowded cities, it offers plenty of green spaces where people can relax. For example, I often enjoy visiting nearby tea gardens, which help me unwind after a busy week.

---

# ❓ Question 3

## What do you not like about it?

### ✅ Answer

> Although I enjoy living there, one drawback is the increasing traffic congestion, especially during peak hours. As the city continues to expand, some roads have become overcrowded. I believe improving the transport system would make daily life much more convenient.

---

# ❓ Question 4

## Is there any way your hometown could be made better?

### ✅ Answer

> Yes, I think there is always room for improvement. If I had the opportunity, I would encourage the local authorities to improve public transportation and create more parks and recreational areas. These changes would enhance the quality of life for both residents and visitors.

---

# ❓ Question 5

## How important is your hometown to you?

### ✅ Answer

> My hometown is extremely important to me because it is where I grew up and created countless memorable experiences with my family and friends. It holds a special place in my heart, and I always feel a sense of comfort whenever I return.

---

# ❓ Question 6

## Can you tell me about your hometown?

### ✅ Answer

> Certainly. Sylhet is a beautiful city that combines natural beauty with a rich cultural heritage. It attracts thousands of visitors every year because of its tea plantations, rivers, and religious landmarks. In my opinion, it is one of the most attractive places to live in Bangladesh.

---

# ❓ Question 7

## How has your hometown changed over the years?

### ✅ Answer

> Over the years, Sylhet has undergone significant development. Many new roads, shopping centres, and residential buildings have been constructed, making the city more modern. At the same time, it has managed to preserve much of its natural beauty, which is something I truly appreciate.

---

# ❓ Question 8

## Do you think you will continue to live in your hometown?

### ✅ Answer

> For the foreseeable future, yes. At the moment, it is the most practical and comfortable place for me to stay. However, I may move abroad in the future if I receive better career opportunities. Even if I relocate, I will always consider Sylhet my true home.

---

# ❓ Question 9

## How often do you visit your hometown?

### ✅ Answer

> Since I currently live in my hometown, I do not need to visit it. However, whenever I travel elsewhere, I always look forward to coming back because it gives me a strong sense of belonging and familiarity.

---

# ❓ Question 10

## How many people live in your hometown?

### ✅ Answer

> Sylhet has a population of several hundred thousand people, making it one of the major cities in Bangladesh. Despite its growing population, it still feels much less hectic than the capital, Dhaka.

---

# ❓ Question 11

## What kind of jobs do the people in your hometown do?

### ✅ Answer

> People in Sylhet work in a wide variety of professions. Many are involved in business, education, healthcare, and government services, while others work in agriculture or the tea industry. In recent years, more young people have also started careers in information technology and freelancing.

---

# ❓ Question 12

## What is your hometown famous for?

### ✅ Answer

> Sylhet is best known for its world-famous tea gardens, breathtaking natural scenery, and religious landmarks. It is also famous for its strong expatriate community, particularly people who have settled in the United Kingdom.

---

# ❓ Question 13

## What's the oldest part of your hometown?

### ✅ Answer

> One of the oldest parts of Sylhet is the area surrounding the shrine of Hazrat Shah Jalal, which has great historical and religious significance. It has existed for centuries and continues to attract both pilgrims and tourists from different parts of the country.

# 🎯 Band 8–9 Tips

- Answer for **20–30 seconds**.
- Follow the **Opinion → Reason → Example → Result** framework.
- Use **2–3 advanced collocations** in every answer.
- Speak naturally instead of memorising.
- Extend your answers with explanations and examples.
- Maintain a confident pace with clear pronunciation.
