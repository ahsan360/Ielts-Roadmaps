# 🎯 Questions & Sample Answers

## ❓ 1. Do you like to take photographs?

**Answer**

> Yes, very much. I enjoy capturing memorable moments, especially when I travel or spend time with my family and friends. Looking back at those photos allows me to relive wonderful experiences.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Capturing
- Memorable moments
- Relive
- Wonderful experiences

---

## ❓ 2. Do you prefer to take photos yourself or to have other people take photos?

**Answer**

> I generally prefer taking photos myself because I enjoy experimenting with different angles and compositions. However, when I am travelling, I sometimes ask someone else to take my picture so I can be part of the memory.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Experimenting
- Angles
- Compositions
- Be part of the memory

---

## ❓ 3. How often do you take photographs?

**Answer**

> I take photographs quite frequently, probably several times a week. Most of the time, I capture interesting places, delicious food, or memorable occasions rather than everyday activities.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Frequently
- Capture
- Memorable occasions

---

## ❓ 4. Do you prefer to take pictures of people or of scenery?

**Answer**

> I enjoy both, but if I had to choose, I would say scenery. Landscapes, mountains, rivers, and sunsets are naturally beautiful, and photographing them helps me appreciate nature even more.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Scenery
- Landscapes
- Appreciate nature

---

## ❓ 5. Do you prefer to take photos with a phone or with a camera?

**Answer**

> Most of the time, I use my smartphone because it is convenient and always within reach. However, if I were photographing a special event or travelling to a scenic destination, I would prefer a professional camera for its superior image quality.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Convenient
- Scenic destination
- Professional camera
- Superior image quality

---

## ❓ 6. Do you take many photographs when you travel?

**Answer**

> Absolutely. Whenever I visit a new place, I take plenty of photographs because I want to preserve those memories. I especially enjoy capturing famous landmarks, local culture, and breathtaking landscapes.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Preserve memories
- Landmarks
- Local culture
- Breathtaking landscapes

---

## ❓ 7. Do you like looking at photographs of yourself?

**Answer**

> Yes, I do, particularly if they remind me of meaningful occasions such as family gatherings or holidays. They bring back pleasant memories and allow me to reflect on how much I have changed over the years.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Meaningful occasions
- Bring back memories
- Reflect on

---

## ❓ 8. Have you put any photographs you have taken on the walls in your house?

**Answer**

> Not yet, but I would certainly like to. I think displaying personal photographs adds warmth and character to a home. If I had more space, I would frame some of my favourite travel photographs and hang them on the walls.

**Band 9 Vocabulary**

- Displaying
- Warmth
- Character
- Frame
