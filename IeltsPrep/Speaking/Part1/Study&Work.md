## 🧠 Speaking Framework

**Opinion → Reason → Example → Result**

---

## ❓1. What are you studying?

### Answer

> I am currently studying Computer Science and Engineering. My course covers subjects such as programming, databases, and software development. I enjoy it because it enables me to solve real-world problems and develop practical skills.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Software development
- Practical skills
- Real-world problems

---

## ❓2. Why did you choose this subject?

### Answer

> I chose this subject because I have always been fascinated by technology. I enjoy creating applications and solving complex problems, so studying computer science was a natural choice for me.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Fascinated
- Complex problems
- Natural choice

---

## ❓3. What is your favourite subject?

### Answer

> My favourite subject is software engineering because it teaches me how to design and build efficient applications. I find it both practical and intellectually stimulating.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Efficient
- Practical
- Intellectually stimulating

---

## ❓4. How do you usually study?

### Answer

> I usually study in a quiet environment where I can concentrate without distractions. I also make notes and revise them regularly to strengthen my understanding.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Concentrate
- Revise
- Strengthen my understanding

---

## ❓5. Do you enjoy studying?

### Answer

> Yes, I do. Although studying can sometimes be demanding, I enjoy learning new concepts because they help me improve both academically and professionally.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Demanding
- Academically
- Professionally

## 🧠 Speaking Framework

**Opinion → Reason → Example → Result**

---

## ❓1. What do you do?

### Answer

> I currently work as a software engineer. My responsibilities include designing, developing, and maintaining web applications. I genuinely enjoy solving technical problems because every project presents a new challenge.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Responsibilities
- Maintaining
- Technical problems

---

## ❓2. Do you enjoy your job?

### Answer

> Yes, I really enjoy my job because it is both challenging and rewarding. Every day, I learn something new, and I have the opportunity to improve my technical skills.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Challenging
- Rewarding
- Technical skills

---

## ❓3. What is the most interesting part of your job?

### Answer

> The most interesting part is solving complex problems and building applications that people use every day. It is satisfying to see the final product working successfully.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Complex problems
- Applications
- Satisfying

---

## ❓4. Do you prefer working alone or with others?

### Answer

> I enjoy both, depending on the task. Working independently allows me to concentrate, while collaborating with colleagues helps me exchange ideas and learn different approaches.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Independently
- Collaborating
- Exchange ideas

---

## ❓5. What kind of job would you like to have in the future?

### Answer

> In the future, I would like to work for a leading technology company where I can contribute to innovative projects, continue learning, and develop my professional expertise.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Innovative
- Contribute
- Professional expertise

# ❌ Common Mistakes

❌ I study computer.

✅ I study **Computer Science**.

---

❌ I work software engineer.

✅ I work **as a software engineer.**

---

❌ My work is very hard.

✅ My work is **quite challenging but rewarding.**

---

❌ I like work with people.

✅ I like **working with people.**
