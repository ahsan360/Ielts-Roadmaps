# 📚 Study

> IELTS Speaking Part 1

---

## ❓1. Do you work or study?

### Answer

> At the moment, I work as a software engineer. However, I recently completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering. Although I have graduated, I still dedicate a significant amount of time to learning new technologies and improving my professional skills.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Bachelor's degree
- Dedicate
- Professional skills
- Improving

---

## ❓2. What is your major? / What was your major?

### Answer

> My major was Computer Science and Engineering. During my studies, I learned about programming, software engineering, databases, algorithms, and computer networks. The course provided me with a solid theoretical foundation and also enabled me to gain practical experience through projects and problem-solving tasks..

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Major
- Theoretical knowledge
- Practical experience
- Algorithms

---

## ❓3. Did you like it?

### Answer

> Yes, I enjoyed it very much. I have always been interested in technology, so I found the course both engaging and rewarding. It also encouraged me to think logically and solve complex problems.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Engaging
- Rewarding
- Logically
- Complex problems

---

## ❓4. Is it a popular major at your university?

### Answer

> Yes, it is one of the most popular majors at my university. Many students choose it because the technology industry is expanding rapidly and offers excellent career opportunities after graduation.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Popular major
- Expanding rapidly
- Career opportunities
- Graduation

---

## ❓5. Why did you choose that major?

### Answer

> I chose Computer Science because I have always been fascinated by technology and problem-solving. I wanted to develop software that could make people's lives easier. It was a perfect combination of creativity and logical thinking.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Fascinated
- Problem-solving
- Creativity
- Logical thinking

---

## ❓6. What is the most difficult part of studying that subject?

### Answer

> I think the most difficult part was understanding complex algorithms and debugging large programs. Some assignments were quite demanding and required a great deal of patience. However, overcoming those challenges made me a better programmer.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Algorithms
- Debugging
- Demanding
- Overcoming challenges

---

## ❓7. Do you plan to use the subject you are studying in the future?

### Answer

> Absolutely. My goal is to build a long-term career as a software engineer. I hope to continue applying the knowledge and skills I gained at university while working on innovative software projects.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Long-term career
- Applying
- Innovative
- Software projects

---

## ❓8. If you could change to another major, what would it be?

### Answer

> If I had to choose another major, I would probably study Artificial Intelligence or Data Science. These fields are developing rapidly and have the potential to solve many real-world problems.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Artificial Intelligence
- Data Science
- Potential
- Real-world problems

---

## ❓9. Would you change it if you had the chance?

### Answer

> No, I wouldn't. I am genuinely satisfied with my decision because Computer Science has opened many career opportunities for me. It perfectly matches my interests and long-term ambitions, so I would definitely choose it again.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

- Genuinely satisfied
- Career opportunities
- Long-term ambitions
- Definitely

---
