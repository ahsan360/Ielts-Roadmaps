# 📚 Study

> IELTS Speaking Part 1 -- Weather

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓1. How is the weather today?

### Answer

> It's sunny today, so I'm in a good mood and it feels like a nice day to go out

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Pleasant
-   Gentle breeze
-   Sunshine
-   Ideal

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓2. What's your favourite kind of weather?

### Answer

> Winter is my favorite season because the cooler weather makes traveling around Bangladesh much easier, while summers can be uncomfortably hot 

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Comfortable
-   Outdoors
-   Exhausted
-   Refreshing

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓3. Is there any type of weather you really don't like?

### Answer

> I don't really like rainy days. They make me feel a bit down and can ruin plans, especially if I wanted to go out.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Humid
-   Productivity
-   Uncomfortable
-   Extremely

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓4. What is the climate like in your country?

### Answer

> Bangladesh has a tropical climate. It's hot and humid most of the year, with a rainy monsoon season that sometimes causes flooding

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Tropical climate
-   Monsoon
-   Mild winters
-   Varies

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓5. Does the weather affect people's lives in your country?

### Answer

> Absolutely. Heavy rainfall can cause flooding, while extreme heat
> affects people's daily routines and work. Farmers are especially
> dependent on weather conditions.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Flooding
-   Daily routines
-   Dependent on
-   Weather conditions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓6. Do people change in the summer?

### Answer

> Yes. During the summer, people usually wear lighter clothes, drink
> more water, and prefer staying indoors during the hottest part of the
> day.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Lighter clothes
-   Stay indoors
-   Hydrated
-   Hottest part

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# ⭐ High-Band Expressions

-   The weather is quite pleasant today.
-   I'm particularly fond of...
-   It makes outdoor activities much more enjoyable.
-   The climate varies throughout the year.
-   Weather has a significant impact on daily life.
-   I prefer mild temperatures.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# ❌ Common Mistakes

❌ The weather is very hotly.

✅ The weather is extremely hot.

❌ I don't like too much rain because I feel bad.

✅ I don't enjoy heavy rainfall because it makes commuting inconvenient.

## ❓7. Does bad weather ever affect transport in your country?

### Answer

> Yes, it does. Heavy rainfall often causes traffic congestion and
> delays, especially in major cities. In some areas, flooding can even
> disrupt public transport.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Traffic congestion
-   Delays
-   Flooding
-   Disrupt

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓8. Tell me about the weather in your country at different times of the year.

### Answer

> Bangladesh experiences four main seasons: summer, the monsoon, autumn,
> and winter. Summers are hot and humid, the monsoon brings heavy
> rainfall, autumn is pleasant, and winters are generally cool and dry.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Experiences
-   Humid
-   Heavy rainfall
-   Cool and dry

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓9. Which time of the year did you enjoy the most when you were a child?

### Answer

> I enjoyed winter the most because the weather was comfortable and
> perfect for outdoor games and family trips. It was also the season of
> picnics and festivals.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Comfortable
-   Outdoor games
-   Festivals
-   Picnics

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓10. Do you usually pay attention to weather forecasts?

### Answer

> Yes. I usually check the weather forecast before travelling or
> planning outdoor activities so that I can avoid unexpected rain or
> extreme heat.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Weather forecast
-   Planning
-   Unexpected
-   Extreme heat

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓11. Has the weather changed much in your country in recent years?

### Answer

> Yes, I think it has become more unpredictable. Summers seem hotter
> than before, and heavy rainfall occurs more frequently due to climate
> change.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Unpredictable
-   Frequently
-   Climate change
-   Heatwaves

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓12. What's the weather usually like in your country (or hometown)?

### Answer

> In my hometown, the weather is generally warm for most of the year.
> During the rainy season, we receive plenty of rainfall, while winter
> is short but quite pleasant.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Generally
-   Rainy season
-   Pleasant
-   Throughout the year

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓13. How is the weather here different from the weather in your home country?

### Answer

> The weather here is generally cooler and less humid than in my home
> country. In Bangladesh, the climate is much warmer and the monsoon
> season brings heavy rainfall.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Less humid
-   Monsoon season
-   Heavy rainfall
-   Climate

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓14. Would you say the weather in your hometown is suitable for working (or studying)?

### Answer

> For most of the year, yes. However, during extremely hot or humid
> days, it can be difficult to concentrate, so many people prefer to
> study or work indoors.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Concentrate
-   Humid
-   Productive
-   Indoors

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓15. Would you like to move to a place with different weather?

### Answer

> Yes, I would like to experience a place with a cooler climate because
> I find cold weather more refreshing and comfortable than extreme heat.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Experience
-   Cooler climate
-   Refreshing
-   Comfortable

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓16. Does the weather affect the way that you feel?

### Answer

Yes, the weather has a noticeable impact on my mood. If it's sunny, I feel excited and really look forward to going out. But if it's rainy or gloomy, I get a bit disappointed because it can spoil my plans. So, yeah, I think weather can change how I feel during the day.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Improves my mood
-   Motivates
-   Gloomy
-   Energetic

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓17. Do people like the weather in your country?

### Answer

> Yes, I think most people like the weather here, but many prefer winter because it's cooler and more comfortable for going out. During the rainy season, heavy rainfall can cause flooding and disrupt daily life

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Depends
-   Intense heat
-   Humidity
-   Complain

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓18. Do the kinds of outside activities people do in your country change when the weather changes?

### Answer

> Absolutely. During pleasant weather, people enjoy playing sports,
> travelling, and spending time in parks. However, when it rains
> heavily, they usually prefer indoor activities such as shopping or
> watching movies.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Outdoor activities
-   Pleasant weather
-   Indoor activities
-   Spend time outdoors

------------------------------------------------------------------------
