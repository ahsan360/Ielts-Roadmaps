# 📚 Study

> IELTS Speaking Part 1 -- Weather

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓1. How is the weather today?

### Answer

> Today the weather is quite pleasant. It's warm with a gentle breeze
> and plenty of sunshine, which makes it an ideal day to spend time
> outdoors.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Pleasant
-   Gentle breeze
-   Sunshine
-   Ideal

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓2. What's your favourite kind of weather?

### Answer

> My favourite weather is cool and sunny because it is comfortable for
> both working and travelling. I also enjoy spending time outdoors
> without feeling exhausted by the heat.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Comfortable
-   Outdoors
-   Exhausted
-   Refreshing

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓3. Is there any type of weather you really don't like?

### Answer

> Yes. I really dislike extremely hot and humid weather because it makes
> me feel tired and uncomfortable. It also reduces my productivity.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Humid
-   Productivity
-   Uncomfortable
-   Extremely

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓4. What is the climate like in your country?

### Answer

> Bangladesh has a tropical climate with hot summers, a rainy monsoon
> season, and relatively mild winters. The weather varies noticeably
> throughout the year.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Tropical climate
-   Monsoon
-   Mild winters
-   Varies

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓5. Does the weather affect people's lives in your country?

### Answer

> Absolutely. Heavy rainfall can cause flooding, while extreme heat
> affects people's daily routines and work. Farmers are especially
> dependent on weather conditions.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Flooding
-   Daily routines
-   Dependent on
-   Weather conditions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓6. Do people change in the summer?

### Answer

> Yes. During the summer, people usually wear lighter clothes, drink
> more water, and prefer staying indoors during the hottest part of the
> day.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Lighter clothes
-   Stay indoors
-   Hydrated
-   Hottest part

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# ⭐ High-Band Expressions

-   The weather is quite pleasant today.
-   I'm particularly fond of...
-   It makes outdoor activities much more enjoyable.
-   The climate varies throughout the year.
-   Weather has a significant impact on daily life.
-   I prefer mild temperatures.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# ❌ Common Mistakes

❌ The weather is very hotly.

✅ The weather is extremely hot.

❌ I don't like too much rain because I feel bad.

✅ I don't enjoy heavy rainfall because it makes commuting inconvenient.

# 📚 Study

> IELTS Speaking Part 1 -- Weather (Part 2)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓7. Does bad weather ever affect transport in your country?

### Answer

> Yes, it does. Heavy rainfall often causes traffic congestion and
> delays, especially in major cities. In some areas, flooding can even
> disrupt public transport.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Traffic congestion
-   Delays
-   Flooding
-   Disrupt

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓8. Tell me about the weather in your country at different times of the year.

### Answer

> Bangladesh experiences four main seasons: summer, the monsoon, autumn,
> and winter. Summers are hot and humid, the monsoon brings heavy
> rainfall, autumn is pleasant, and winters are generally cool and dry.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Experiences
-   Humid
-   Heavy rainfall
-   Cool and dry

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓9. Which time of the year did you enjoy the most when you were a child?

### Answer

> I enjoyed winter the most because the weather was comfortable and
> perfect for outdoor games and family trips. It was also the season of
> picnics and festivals.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Comfortable
-   Outdoor games
-   Festivals
-   Picnics

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓10. Do you usually pay attention to weather forecasts?

### Answer

> Yes. I usually check the weather forecast before travelling or
> planning outdoor activities so that I can avoid unexpected rain or
> extreme heat.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Weather forecast
-   Planning
-   Unexpected
-   Extreme heat

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓11. Has the weather changed much in your country in recent years?

### Answer

> Yes, I think it has become more unpredictable. Summers seem hotter
> than before, and heavy rainfall occurs more frequently due to climate
> change.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Unpredictable
-   Frequently
-   Climate change
-   Heatwaves

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ❓12. What's the weather usually like in your country (or hometown)?

### Answer

> In my hometown, the weather is generally warm for most of the year.
> During the rainy season, we receive plenty of rainfall, while winter
> is short but quite pleasant.

### Band 7+ Vocabulary

-   Generally
-   Rainy season
-   Pleasant
-   Throughout the year

------------------------------------------------------------------------
