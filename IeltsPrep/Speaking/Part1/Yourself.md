| Word | Meaning |

| Adaptable | Able to adjust to new situations |

# 🎯 Questions & Sample Answers

---

## ❓ 1. Can you describe yourself?

### Answer

> I would describe myself as an ambitious and curious person who enjoys learning new things. I am particularly passionate about technology and constantly try to improve my skills. I also enjoy travelling because it allows me to broaden my horizons and experience different cultures.

### Band 9 Vocabulary

- Ambitious
- Curious
- Passionate
- Broaden my horizons

---

## ❓ 2. What do you do?

### Answer

> At the moment, I work as a software engineer. My job mainly involves designing, developing, and maintaining web applications. I genuinely enjoy solving challenging problems because every project gives me an opportunity to learn something new.

### Band 9 Vocabulary

- Designing
- Developing
- Maintaining
- Challenging problems

---

## ❓ 3. What do you usually do in your leisure time?

### Answer

> In my leisure time, I usually watch educational videos, practise English, or explore new technologies. Occasionally, I travel to scenic places with my friends. These activities help me relax while also improving my knowledge and creativity.

### Band 9 Vocabulary

- Leisure time
- Educational videos
- Explore
- Creativity

---

## ❓ 4. What are your hobbies?

### Answer

> My favourite hobbies include travelling, photography, and learning about new technologies. I also enjoy solving programming problems because they sharpen my logical thinking and improve my problem-solving abilities.

### Band 9 Vocabulary

- Sharpen
- Logical thinking
- Problem-solving abilities

---

## ❓ 5. Are you a busy person?

### Answer

> Yes, I am quite busy most of the time. I usually spend my day working, studying, and improving my professional skills. However, I always try to maintain a healthy balance between work and relaxation.

### Band 9 Vocabulary

- Maintain
- Professional skills
- Healthy balance

---

## ❓ 6. What do you enjoy doing with your friends?

### Answer

> Whenever I meet my friends, we usually go out for dinner, visit interesting places, or simply have long conversations about technology and life. Spending time with them helps me relax and strengthens our friendship.

### Band 9 Vocabulary

- Strengthens
- Interesting places
- Long conversations

---

## ❓ 7. What kind of person would you like to become?

### Answer

> I would like to become a highly skilled software engineer who contributes to innovative projects. I also hope to become more confident in English so that I can communicate effectively with people from different countries.

### Band 9 Vocabulary

- Highly skilled
- Innovative
- Communicate effectively

---

## ❓ 8. What are your future goals?

### Answer

> One of my long-term goals is to work for a leading technology company such as Google. I also hope to travel extensively, experience different cultures, and continue developing both my technical expertise and communication skills.

### Band 9 Vocabulary

- Long-term goals
- Extensively
- Technical expertise

---

## ❓ 9. What makes you happy?

### Answer

> I feel happiest when I achieve something meaningful after putting in consistent effort. It gives me a strong sense of accomplishment and motivates me to work even harder towards my future goals.

### Band 9 Vocabulary

- Meaningful
- Consistent effort
- Sense of accomplishment

---

## ❓ 10. Are you an organised person?

### Answer

> Yes, I consider myself quite organised. I usually plan my day in advance and prioritise the most important tasks first. This habit helps me stay productive and manage my time efficiently.

### Band 9 Vocabulary

- Organised
- Prioritise
- Efficiently

---

## ❓ 11. What do you usually do at the weekend?

### Answer

> I usually spend my weekends relaxing, catching up with friends, exploring new places, or working on personal projects. It helps me recharge and prepare for the week ahead.

### Band 9 Vocabulary

- Catch up with friends
- Personal projects
- Recharge

---

## ❓ 12. How do you usually spend your evenings?

### Answer

> I normally spend my evenings reading, practising English, or learning new programming concepts. Before going to bed, I usually plan my tasks for the following day.

### Band 9 Vocabulary

- Programming concepts
- Following day
- Productive routine

## ❌ Common Mistakes

❌ **I am software engineer.**

✅ **I am a software engineer.**

---

❌ **I like learn new things.**

✅ **I like learning new things.**

---

❌ **In free time I watching YouTube.**

✅ **In my free time, I usually watch YouTube.**

---

❌ **I want work Google.**

✅ **I want to work at Google.**

---

❌ **I am very busy always.**

✅ **I am usually quite busy with my work and studies.**
