# IELTS Speaking Part 2 (Band 7.5+)

## Cue Card

**Describe a piece of technology (not a mobile phone) that you find useful.**

You should say:

- What it is
- When you started using it
- How you use it
- And explain why you find it useful.

---

# Sample Answer (2 Minutes)

I'd like to talk about my **smartphone**, which is undoubtedly the piece of technology I find most useful in my everyday life. Although many people take smartphones for granted these days, I genuinely can't imagine my daily routine without one.

I first started using a smartphone when I was in **high school**. At that time, I mainly used it for making calls, sending messages, and browsing the internet. However, as technology evolved and my needs changed, it gradually became much more than just a communication device. Today, I rely on it for both my personal life and my professional work as a software engineer.

One of the main reasons I find it so useful is that it helps me **stay organised and productive**. For example, I use it to check my emails, manage my calendar, create to-do lists, and keep track of important deadlines. Instead of relying on paper notes, I can store everything digitally and access it whenever I need it.

Another way I use my smartphone is for **navigation**. Whenever I visit a new place, I simply open Google Maps to find the quickest route. It not only saves me time but also prevents me from getting lost, especially when I'm travelling to unfamiliar cities.

Communication is another area where my smartphone plays a vital role. I regularly use applications like **WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and Telegram** to stay in touch with my family, friends, and colleagues. If necessary, I can make voice or video calls from virtually anywhere, which makes communication incredibly convenient.

Apart from that, my smartphone is also an excellent learning tool. I frequently read technical blogs, watch programming tutorials on YouTube, and practise English using various language-learning apps. As someone who works in the technology industry, I believe continuous learning is essential, and my smartphone allows me to learn even when I'm away from my computer.

It also helps me stay informed about current affairs. I regularly check news applications and trustworthy websites to keep myself updated on what's happening around the world. During my free time, I listen to podcasts, read e-books, or watch documentaries, which makes my leisure time both enjoyable and productive.

Overall, I think my smartphone has become an indispensable part of my life. It enables me to communicate effectively, work efficiently, learn new skills, navigate unfamiliar places, and stay informed. That's why I consider it one of the most valuable pieces of technology I own.

# Speaking Structure

1. **Introduce the technology**
2. **When you started using it**
3. **How you use it for work**
4. **How you use it for communication**
5. **How you use it for learning**
6. **How you use it while travelling**
7. **How it helps in daily life**
8. **Conclusion (Why it's valuable)**
