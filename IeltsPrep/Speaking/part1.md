1. Tell me about the kind of accommodation you live in.
I currently live in a family house in Sylhet. It is a quiet and comfortable place where I have been living with my family for many years. What I particularly like about it is the peaceful atmosphere, as it allows me to focus on both my work and personal studies without much distraction.

2. Do you live in a house or a flat?
I live in a house. To be honest, I prefer living in a house because it offers more space and privacy compared to a flat. It also feels more relaxing since there is less noise from neighbours.

3. Is it a big place?
Yes, I would say it is fairly spacious. It has several bedrooms, a living room, a dining area, and a small outdoor space. It is not enormous, but it is certainly large enough for my family to live comfortably.

4. Does the place you live in have many amenities?
Yes, it does. We have all the basic facilities such as electricity, clean water, and a stable internet connection, which is especially important for me as I work online. Besides that, there are shops, schools, and hospitals nearby, so everything is easily accessible.

5. What do you like about living there?
What I like most is the sense of comfort and familiarity. Since I have spent most of my life there, I feel emotionally connected to the place. On top of that, the neighbourhood is calm and friendly, which makes living there even more enjoyable.

6. Is there anything you would like to change about the place you live in?
Yes, if I had the chance, I would probably redesign my room to make it more modern and functional. For instance, I would like to have better lighting and a larger workspace because I spend a significant amount of time studying and working on my computer.

7. How long have you lived there?
I have been living there since my childhood, so it has been more than twenty years now. Over time, I have created countless memories there, which makes it a very special place for me.

8. Do you plan to live there for a long time?
For the foreseeable future, yes. At the moment, it is the most practical and comfortable place for me to stay. However, I might relocate in the future if I get better career opportunities abroad.

9. Is there a garden in the place you live in?
Yes, there is a small garden in front of our house. It is not very large, but we grow a few flowers and plants there. I sometimes spend time there in the evening because it is a good way to relax and clear my mind.

1. Do you like watching advertisements?
Yes, provided they are creative and informative. I particularly enjoy advertisements that tell a compelling story or incorporate humour. However, I usually skip repetitive or overly promotional ones because they tend to become monotonous.

2. Will you buy something because of an advertisement?
Advertisements may capture my attention, but I rarely purchase a product solely because of one. I prefer to read customer reviews, compare different options, and ensure the product offers good value for money before making a decision.

3. How do you feel when you see pop-up ads on the internet?
To be honest, I find them rather irritating because they interrupt whatever I am doing. They can also slow down websites and distract me from my original purpose. Therefore, I usually close them immediately or use an ad blocker.

4. Do you like funny or serious advertisements?
I definitely prefer funny advertisements. Humorous commercials are far more memorable and entertaining, and they create a positive impression of the brand. Serious advertisements are suitable for important issues, but they are generally less engaging.

5. What makes a good advertisement?
In my opinion, a good advertisement should be concise, visually appealing, and informative. It should clearly highlight the product's benefits without exaggerating. Creativity and authenticity also play a significant role in making an advertisement memorable.

6. Do you like art?
Yes, I do, although I would not describe myself as an expert. I appreciate artworks that convey emotions or tell meaningful stories. Visiting art exhibitions occasionally helps me relax and broadens my perspective.

7. Do you think art classes are necessary?
Yes, I believe they are essential. Art classes encourage students to think creatively, express their emotions, and develop problem-solving skills. They also provide a welcome break from purely academic subjects.

8. How do you think art classes affect children's development?
I think art classes contribute significantly to children's overall development. They enhance creativity, improve concentration, and strengthen fine motor skills. Moreover, they encourage children to communicate their thoughts confidently and think independently.

9. What benefits can you get from painting as a hobby?
Painting can be incredibly therapeutic. It helps reduce stress, stimulates creativity, and improves patience. In addition, completing a painting gives a great sense of accomplishment and boosts self-confidence.

10. Do you like to take photographs?
Yes, very much. I enjoy capturing memorable moments, especially when I travel or spend time with my family and friends. Looking back at those photos allows me to relive wonderful experiences.

11. Do you prefer to take photos yourself or to have other people take photos?
I generally prefer taking photos myself because I enjoy experimenting with different angles and compositions. However, when I am travelling, I sometimes ask someone else to take my picture so I can be part of the memory.

12. How often do you take photographs?
I take photographs quite frequently, probably several times a week. Most of the time, I capture interesting places, delicious food, or memorable occasions rather than everyday activities.

13. Do you prefer to take pictures of people or of scenery?
I enjoy both, but if I had to choose, I would say scenery. Landscapes, mountains, rivers, and sunsets are naturally beautiful, and photographing them helps me appreciate nature even more.

14. Do you prefer to take photos with a phone or with a camera?
Most of the time, I use my smartphone because it is convenient and always within reach. However, if I were photographing a special event or travelling to a scenic destination, I would prefer a professional camera for its superior image quality.

15. Do you take many photographs when you travel?
Absolutely. Whenever I visit a new place, I take plenty of photographs because I want to preserve those memories. I especially enjoy capturing famous landmarks, local culture, and breathtaking landscapes.

16. Do you like looking at photographs of yourself?
Yes, I do, particularly if they remind me of meaningful occasions such as family gatherings or holidays. They bring back pleasant memories and allow me to reflect on how much I have changed over the years.

17. Have you put any photographs you have taken on the walls in your house?
Not yet, but I would certainly like to. I think displaying personal photographs adds warmth and character to a home. If I had more space, I would frame some of my favourite travel photographs and hang them on the walls.
