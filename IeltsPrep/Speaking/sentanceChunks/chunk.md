# IELTS Speaking Band 8+ Universal Sentence Chunks

> **Goal:** Memorise these chunks, not full answers. Use them naturally in **Part 1, Part 2, and Part 3**.

# Band 8 Answer Framework (Part 1)

Opinion

↓

Reason

↓

Example

↓

Feeling / Result

**Formula:**

Opinion → Reason → Example → Feeling

**Example:**

> Personally, I enjoy reading books because they help me relax after a busy day. For example, I usually read for about half an hour before going to bed. It gives me a sense of peace and helps me clear my mind.
